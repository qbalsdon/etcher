1. Forgot to connect the power adapter to the machine to power on the machine.
Solution: Connect the machine and power on it.

2. Power adapter is broken.
Solution: Replace a new power adapter.

3. Firmware is broken (The the indicator light could be on, after power on)
Solution: Reinstall the firmware again (Please contact with us to get the correct firmware then).

4. Motherboard is burned (The the indicator light is not on, after power on)
Solution: Replace a new motherboard.
