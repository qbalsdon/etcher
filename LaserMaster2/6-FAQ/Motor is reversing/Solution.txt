1. The settings of firmware had been changed via the control software, using the console commands.
Solution is to reset the factory setting:
https://github.com/gnea/grbl/wiki/Grbl-v1.1-Configuration 
https://github.com/gnea/grbl/wiki/Grbl-v1.1-Commands 
$RST=$, $RST=#, and $RST=*- Restore Grbl settings and data to defaults

2. Wrong firmware had been re-installed by mistake.
Solution:  is to re-install the correct firmware.

3. Wiring sequence of motor wires.
Solution is to exchange the Wiring sequence of motor wires.
